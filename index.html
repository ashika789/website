    Business Vision

    • Build capabilities into these core platforms and frameworks to support the Delivery of the Client Experience Standard and a Goals Based Wealth Management Process – Other platforms will be retired over time
    • Enable profiling tools to be accessed or shared across platform at the advisors discretion.
    • Enable Digital Goals Based Service Experience
    • Create a design and experience that enables advisors to move more seamlessly across “shelf space” platforms
    • Lead the industry in mobile and online services for Clients AND Advisors enabling next generation client/advisor collaboration and industry leading online and mobile experience for Clients, Prospects and Advisors.
    • Drives simplification of interfaces and rationalization of existing platforms.
    • Re-envision our online and mobile experiences as Service and Collaboration Centers – An Extension of the Advisor Team

Technology Principles

  • Connect the platforms so that application development practices are standardized by leveraging core set of common capabilities to enable seamless collaboration
  • Applications should be thought of as a set of reusable, device/channel agnostic UI components that can be easily assembled to provide the consistent user experience
  • Applications must follow Responsive design so that the application layout is flexible and fluid adapting to the layout of the device's screen
  • Applications must follow Adaptive design so that user experience is tailored specifically to take advantage of device/channel specific features
  • Ensure that every touch point business solution has been reviewed from a Mobile First perspective and automatically trigger architecture reviews
